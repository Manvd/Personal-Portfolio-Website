Common questions

Before you reach out

Quick answers to the questions I get most often. Still have something else? Just send a message above.

What types of projects do you take on?
+
I specialise in data engineering and Python backend work — ETL/ELT pipelines, data warehouses, REST APIs, distributed task systems, and cloud infrastructure. I take on projects end-to-end or as a specialist in a specific layer.
What is your typical engagement model?
+
I work on fixed-scope projects (defined deliverables, fixed price) and ongoing retainer contracts (hourly or monthly). For larger projects I prefer starting with a paid discovery sprint to define scope, architecture, and data contracts before committing to a full estimate.
How long does a typical project take?
+
A focused API or pipeline can be done in 2–4 weeks. A full data warehouse with orchestration and quality monitoring typically takes 6–12 weeks. I share a realistic timeline after the discovery phase — I don't overpromise.
Are you open to full-time opportunities?
+
Yes — I'm open to full-time remote roles as a Data Engineer or Python Backend Developer. I'm looking for teams that care about engineering quality, have interesting data problems, and value clear communication.
Do you work with international clients?
+
Absolutely. I've worked with clients across India, Europe, and North America. I'm comfortable with async communication, overlap hours, and tools like Slack, Notion, Linear, and Jira. Time zones are rarely a blocker if communication is clear.
